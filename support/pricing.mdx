## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change my plan anytime?">
    Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do you offer discounts for non-profits?">
    Yes, we offer 25% discounts for qualified non-profit organizations. Contact our sales team with your non-profit documentation for more information.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my plan limits?">
    We'll notify you when you're approaching your plan limits (at 80% and 95%). You can upgrade your plan or purchase additional capacity. We won't cut off your access - we'll work with you to find the right solution.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a setup fee?">
    No setup fees for any plan. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden costs. All pricing is clearly stated upfront.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How is Enterprise pricing determined?">
    Enterprise pricing is based on the number of locations, users, integrations, and specific requirements. We provide custom quotes based on your needs. Contact our sales team for a detailed proposal.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do you offer volume discounts?">
    Yes, we offer volume discounts for large restaurant chains. Discounts start at 10+ locations and increase with scale. Contact sales for custom pricing.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I get a custom contract?">
    Yes, Enterprise customers can have custom contracts with specific terms and conditions, including custom SLAs, payment terms, and feature commitments.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    We accept credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), ACH transfers, wire transfers, and purchase orders (Enterprise only). All payments are processed securely.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I pause my subscription?">
    We don't offer subscription pausing, but you can downgrade to the Starter plan if you need to reduce costs temporarily. You can upgrade again at any time.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do you offer educational discounts?">
    Yes, we offer discounts for culinary schools and educational institutions. Contact our sales team with your educational institution details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
