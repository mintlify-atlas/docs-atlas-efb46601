## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="🎯 AI-Powered Demand Forecasting" icon="brain">
    Our machine learning models analyze historical sales data, weather patterns, local events, and seasonal trends to predict salsa demand 7-30 days in advance with 95% accuracy. This helps you:
    
    - Reduce waste by up to 60%
    - Optimize production schedules
    - Prevent stockouts during peak periods
    - Lower ingredient costs through better planning
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="📡 Real-Time IoT Quality Monitoring" icon="signal">
    Connect temperature sensors, RFID tags, and quality meters to monitor freshness and consistency automatically:
    
    - Temperature and humidity tracking
    - Automated freshness scoring
    - Quality alerts and anomaly detection
    - Compliance reporting for HACCP and FDA
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="📊 Advanced Analytics" icon="chart-line">
    Get actionable insights from your production data:
    
    - Production efficiency metrics
    - Waste analysis and cost optimization
    - Recipe performance comparisons
    - Custom reports and dashboards
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="🔗 Seamless Integrations" icon="plug">
    Connect with your existing systems:
    
    - 50+ POS integrations (Square, Toast, Clover)
    - ERP systems (SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics)
    - Supply chain platforms
    - Direct supplier ordering
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="🏢 Enterprise Ready" icon="building">
